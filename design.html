About the project

Clandestine Yoga was an emerging yoga "studio" where, for each class, a group of people would list to attend the class without knowing where would it take place, then all the particpants would meet in a given place and from there, they all would be given a groupal ride to the final destination where the yoga class would take place, that`s why the name of the brand has the word "clandestine" in it.
So I played with that concept and designed the logo.

02

The logo

The logo has a couple of hidden meanings. The first one, is that the whole logo is basically a triangle, and the triangle is a pretty synthesized representation of a mountain. I though about using a mountain because it is the name of one of the most basic yoga poses ("tadasana" - which means mountain). The green part is also a triangle-shapped "C" letter (Clandestine), and the brown part is a "Y" letter (yoga) that kind of tries to be hidden in the mountain representing the clandestine concept. So to sume up, the concept of the logo is yoga and hide.
